Augmented Reality

Explanation

Augmented Reality: augmented is another word for "enhanced". One enhances the real world with computer gernerated objects to help you or to entertain you. You add virtual objects into the real world with the help of special technology.

Evolution of Augmented Reality

In 1901 L. Frank Baum first has the idea for Augmented Reality.

The first AR-System was created by Louis Rosenberg with the name "Virtual Fixtures" at the United States Air Force Research Laboratory.

Augmented Reality head up displays in fighter jets were the earliest use case of AR-Technology and are still used today.
